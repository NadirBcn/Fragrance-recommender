    Item 2577443

    NewOnly at Sephora

    What it is: A hair and body mist with notes of Brazilian jasmine and vanilla.

    Fragrance Family: Florals

    Scent Type: Fruity Florals

    Key Notes: Brazilian Jasmine, Pink Dragonfruit, Sheer Vanilla

    Fragrance Description: In Brazil, one essential beauty secret is to be "cheirosa" (sheh-ro-zah), which means to always smell delicious. Vibrant and unexpected, this fruity-floral scent offers a transportive temptation through the abundant, sun-dappled gardens of Rio. This immersive scent is kissed with joy and always inviting.

    About the Fragrance: This fresh, bright floral scent invites you into Sol de Janeiro’s lush garden by the sea—a sun-dappled oasis brimming with vibrant flora and dancing with ocean air.

    Suggested Usage:
    -Take cheirosa to the next level.
    -Spray this mist anywhere and everywhere, including your body, hair, clothes, and even your home.
    -Mist all the time for a lingering scent of Brazilian jasmine, pink dragonfruit, and sheer vanilla.

    Recycling Instructions:
    -Separate cap and pump from the bottle and place in recycling.
